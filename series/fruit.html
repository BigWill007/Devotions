A 7-day journey into bearing spiritual fruit

Based on John 15:1-17

Introduction

This devotional explores what it means to bear fruit as a follower of Jesus Christ. Over the next seven days, we'll discover the profound truths from John 15:1-17 about our connection to Jesus as the true vine and how this relationship produces spiritual fruit in our lives. Each day includes Scripture from the ESV Bible, a devotional reflection, questions for personal application, a guided prayer, and a practical step to take.

Whether you're just beginning your journey with Christ or have been walking with Him for years, this devotional will help you understand what it means to abide in Jesus and bear the fruit that glorifies God and demonstrates true discipleship.
