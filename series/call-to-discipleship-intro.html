Introduction

This 7-day devotional explores what it means to be a disciple of Jesus Christ. Through daily readings from the Gospel of Matthew, we'll discover how Jesus calls ordinary people to follow Him and how we can respond to that call in our own lives. Each day includes Scripture from the ESV Bible, a devotional reflection, questions for personal application, a guided prayer, and a practical step to take.

Whether you're just beginning your journey with Jesus or have been following Him for years, this devotional will help you understand what true discipleship looks like and encourage you to take your next step of faith.
