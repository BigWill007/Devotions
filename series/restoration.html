Introduction

This devotional explores the journey from failure to restoration through the story of Peter in John 21. Over the next seven days, we'll discover the profound truths about how God restores us when we fall short. Each day includes Scripture from the ESV Bible, a devotional reflection, questions for personal application, a guided prayer, and a practical step to take.

Whether you're currently experiencing the pain of failure or seeking to help someone who is, this devotional will guide you through the biblical pathway to restoration. Peter's story reminds us that our failures are never final when we follow God's process for healing and renewal.
