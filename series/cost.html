Introduction

This devotional explores what it means to count the cost of following Jesus. Over the next seven days, we'll discover the profound truths from Matthew 16 about what discipleship cost Jesus and what it costs us. Each day includes Scripture from the ESV Bible, a devotional reflection, questions for personal application, a guided prayer, and a practical step to take.

Whether you're just beginning your journey with Jesus or have been following Him for years, this devotional will help you understand the cost and value of true discipleship and encourage you to embrace the life-giving path of following Christ wholeheartedly.
